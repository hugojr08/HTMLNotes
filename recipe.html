Equipment

  • Toaster
  • butter knife/Spoon
  • Knife
  • Plate

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Take out all of your ingredients and equipment
  2. Toast 2 slices of bread at your liking
  3. Use your butter knife to spread mayonnaise on both slices of bread
  4. Add your ham and piece of cheese
  5. Then use your knife to slice pieces of tomato and lettuce
  6. Add your slices of vegetables to your sandwich
  7. You can have a drink aside but if not now you can enjoy your sandwich on its plate
